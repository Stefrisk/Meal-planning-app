Effortless Meal Plans with Optimal Nutrition

How to use NutriPlan

  1. Select a day of the week obs! the weekday will stay highlighted, it is now the active day of the week on the page!
  2. Create a new meal or search for a existing meal
  3. Build your meal then customize the serving size of ingredients to your needs
  4. Add meal to your chosen day ( OBS! 1-10 meals or snacks can be added per day!)
  5. When you feel your meal plan for that day is complete, add it to your week and continue with the next day.
